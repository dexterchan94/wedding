About the Venue

Instead of a traditional wedding venue we’ve opted for something much more intimate and simple - we’ve rented and Airbnb in Britannia Beach. From roasting s’mores in a fire-pit to watching the sunset on the balcony, it’s going to be a fun night.

How to Get There

Driving Instructions

  • - Merge onto BC-99N
  • - Get on Trans-Canada Hwy (BC-99)
  • - Follow BC-99 to Cooper Dr in Britannia Beach
  • - Follow Cooper Dr to Lower Crescent

Recommendations

  • - Parking is limited so we recommend carpooling and assigning a designated driver
  • - Give yourself plenty of time to get there
